About MealBridge

🚨 The Problem

Millions of meals are wasted every day while many people struggle to access nutritious food. NGOs often don't know where surplus food is available or which donation should be collected first.

💡 Our Solution

MealBridge connects restaurants with NGOs by providing a platform where surplus food can be shared quickly and efficiently, reducing food waste and helping communities.

⚙️ How MealBridge Works

Restaurants upload surplus food details. NGOs can view nearby donations, identify urgent pickups, accept requests, and follow optimized routes for collection.

🤖 AI in Action

MealBridge intelligently identifies food from uploaded images, estimates servings, predicts freshness, prioritizes donations based on urgency, and suggests efficient pickup routes.

🌍 Our Impact

Our mission is to reduce food waste, support NGOs, improve food accessibility, and build a smarter, AI-powered food rescue ecosystem.
